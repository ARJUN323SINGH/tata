TATA Group

The Tata Group (/ˈtɑːtɑː/) is a group of companies headquartered in Mumbai, India.[4][5] Established in 1868, it is India's largest conglomerate, with products and services in over 150 countries, and operations in 100 countries.

About

There are 29 publicly listed Tata Group companies with a combined market capitalisation of ₹31.6 trillion (US$382 billion) as of 8 March 2024.[7] Major affiliates include Tata Consultancy Services, Tata Motors, Tata Projects, Tata Power, Titan, Tata Steel, Air India, Indian Hotels Company, Tata Consumer Products, Voltas, Trent, Cromā and BigBasket
